fermat little theorem:
    a ^ (m-1) = 1 (mod m) whem m is prime and a is not divisible by m
    a ^ b (mod m) = a ^ (b (mod m-1)) (mod m)

product of divisors:    n ^ (number of divisors/2) [n is not a perfect square]
                        n ^ ((number of divisors-1)/2) * sqrt(n) [n is a perfect square]
